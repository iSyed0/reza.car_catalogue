Persona 2

A man wants to bet on a quarter-mile race which is held between several different cars. He wants to pre-determine which is the most likely candidate to win the race.

  • For this task, he will use the app to enter betting-mode.
  • Here, the user will input the length of the track, and the cars.
  • The app will take note of the cars' acceleration-rates, Chasis-weight, engine-power and calculate the chances of victory from best to worst.
