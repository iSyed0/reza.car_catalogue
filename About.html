About



A sanctuary for car enthusiasts and other people looking to obtain information to help them easily compare the best automobiles to be able to buy something more suited to their requirements. This app has been developed with the intention of being inviting with its simplistic, yet informative architecture. We aim to cater to users who are looking for car specs/prices/nationality to discussing information with other users while logged in to the app as well as people looking to stay updated with the latest car updates in the blogs section.
